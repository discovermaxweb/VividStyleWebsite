Company Intro and Goals About Us

Vivid Style Trade 304CC was started in April 2006 with one 6 MTR Truck and has grown to a wide range of vehicles and construction equipment for hire. We expect even greater growth in the years to follow.

We at Vivid Style feel that it is very important to be part of the business sector in South Africa. We understand the importance of job creation and therefore strive to create as many jobs as possible within the company.

We provide quick and professional service and will always go the extra mile to get the job done.
